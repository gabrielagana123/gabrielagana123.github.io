About Me

I'm Gabriel Agana Anongwin, Doctor of Pharmacy, KNUST class of 2026 (Rx26), and an ALX certified Data Analyst currently specialiazing in Machine learning. I am a benefeciary of the HAS scholarship, funded by the MD of Fidelity Bank, Ghana and his philantropists colleagues. I am a multi grant winner; from the Mstecard SBS program, I won a grant to work on my startup Renolab, where we focus on the use of Machine Learning for the early detection of Kidney disease. And another grant from Mastercard Foundation SEF KNUST, for a startup called SAUTIq, a web design and multimedia studio where we focus on giving digital and online presence to small buisness owners and entepreneuers.

What drives me is impact. Losing my father at 11 and being supported by people outside my family shaped how I see life. It taught me that, "I am because we are" and that no meaningful success is ever truly individual. Every pursuit, evry goal, every ambition, every project I embark on is impact driven, my goal in life is to touch as many lives as possible and give my single mom the life she never had. "I Love The People Society Casts Away❤️"
